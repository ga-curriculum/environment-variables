## About

This module on Environment Variables is designed for both beginner and intermediate learners who aim to understand and effectively implement environment variables in their projects. The content is tailored to demystify the concept of environment variables, illustrating their critical role in differentiating between development and production environments. It emphasizes the importance of environment variables in managing sensitive data, such as API keys and database passwords, securely.
